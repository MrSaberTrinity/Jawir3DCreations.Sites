Guardian Tales Addon

For Minecraft Bedrock Addon

Experience the thrill of exploring the Minecraft world with a magical touch from the popular RPG "Guardian Tales"! This addon brings iconic heroes into the blocky realm of Minecraft Bedrock Edition, complete with special abilities that you can use directly. Transform into your favorite character, activate their unique skills, and conquer Minecraft adventures with the signature Guardian Tales style!

Features

  • 7+ Playable Characters - Transform and play as iconic characters:
    • The Knight
    • Dr. Plague
    • Saya
    • Wakamo
    • Andrass
    • Vinette
    • Beth
  • 3D Weapons with Unique Skills - Each weapon has different visual effects and special abilities
  • New Bosses & Minions - Face challenging enemies from the world of Guardian Tales
  • Hidden Features - Discover easter eggs and secret content waiting to be explored!
